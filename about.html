About EduOverseaz

Empowering Dreams Through Global Education

For over 13 years, EduOverseaz has been the trusted companion for students and professionals embarking on international education journeys. From our headquarters in Karol Bagh (New Delhi), we transform aspirations into achievements—one student at a time.

Who We Are

EduOverseaz is more than an overseas education consultancy—we are partners in helping you experience world-class learning beyond borders. Our seasoned consultants bring deep expertise in global education systems, visa regulations, and cross-cultural transitions.

We specialize in study abroad consulting and visa services for Canada, Australia, the United Kingdom, the United States, Europe, and exclusive pathway programs via Dubai and Singapore.

Mission

To simplify overseas education and visa processes through transparent, reliable, and personalized services that empower our clients to pursue academic and professional success with confidence.

Vision

To be recognized as India's most trusted and student-centric overseas education consultancy—known for integrity, expertise, and unwavering commitment to student success.

What Sets Us Apart

Strategic Global Partnerships

We have cultivated strong alliances with leading universities and education providers across continents. These collaborations unlock access to institutions that match your academic goals, budget, and long-term plans.

Pathway Programs Advantage

Our Dubai and Singapore pathway routes provide a flexible, affordable launchpad to prestigious universities worldwide.

  • ✓ Cost-effective: lower tuition and living expenses in the foundation years.
  • ✓ Cultural adaptation: gradual transition into international education systems.
  • ✓ Quality assurance: high academic standards in globally connected hubs.
  • ✓ Flexibility: multiple pathways to universities in the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, and Europe.

Personalized Counseling Approach

Every learner’s journey is unique. We invest time in understanding your academic background, career ambitions, financial parameters, and personal preferences before recommending countries, universities, and courses.

End-to-End Support

We stand beside you from the first counseling session to post-arrival support overseas.

  • • Pre-departure: country & university selection, application filing, visa assistance, loan facilitation.
  • • Documentation: meticulous preparation to maximize admission and visa success.
  • • Post-arrival: accommodation, orientation, and continued support as you settle abroad.

Transparency & Integrity

No hidden charges, no false promises, and no aggressive sales tactics. We build trust through honest assessments, realistic expectations, and ethical guidance—keeping you informed at every step.
